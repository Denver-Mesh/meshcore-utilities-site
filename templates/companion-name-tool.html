How to set your keys:

  1. Download your configuration file below and copy it to your phone.
  2. Open the MeshCore app on your phone and connect your companion device.
  3. Click the gear icon (⚙️) and scroll down to "Import Config" under "Extra Tools".
  4. Select the JSON file you downloaded, then check "Name", "Private Identity Key" and "Radio Settings". Make sure all other settings are unchecked.
  5. Click the check icon (✔️) at the top of the screen to apply the settings.

This process will update your device's name, public key and private key, effectively giving it a new identity on the mesh.
